American Idol: Haley Slaton Gets Golden Ticket- Expectant Mother Meets His Husband On The Show, Ever

Expectant mother, Haley Slaton, gets the golden ticket in American Idol 2022 as the judges give their yesses.

It is the preliminary audition round of the American Idol 2022 and we are already seeing some incredible talents.

With three top-notch judges to refine the participants, multiple singers have already impressed the audience.

One of them is Slaton who appeared in the recent episode and impressed the viewers with her voice as well as her charms.

She also opened up about expecting a baby and what’s exciting is the fact that she met her now-husband only in the actual audition of this program in 2021.

Haley Slaton Gets Golden Ticket: Expectant Mother Performance

Haley Slaton finally got her Golden Ticket in American Idol after she managed to impress the judges with her second song.

She first sang The Climb by Miley Cyrus but it seemed like the judges were not fully convinced on her notes.

Since she was an expectant mother, they gave her another chance to sing the other song that she had brought with her.

And well, Haley gave her everything knowing that it would be her last chance.

For the second song, she sang One and Only by Adele and she absolutely killed it.

All three judges found it much better than the first and eventually gave three yesses.

How Old Is Haley Slaton? Age Details

The expectant mother on American Idol, Haley Slaton is 23 years old.

Before showing the actual audition, the program shows the introduction video and other details of its contestants.

In that part, Haley was mentioned to be at the age of 23.

From that information, we can trace down her birth year to be around 1998/99 but her actual date of birth is unknown.

Who Is Haley Slaton Husband?

Haley Slaton’s husband is Myles D. Jordan.

Slaton and her spouse, Jordan, met for the first time during their audition at the beginning of 2021. The audition for the 20th season took place in February and March.

Apparently, they liked each other and dated for a few months before officially tying the knot in December of 2021.

The couple married on Christmas Eve and eventually welcomed their first child in January of 2022.

More on her partner, Jordan played football previously while he currently seems to be focused on singing and other activities.

Who Are Haley Slaton Parents?

Talking about Haley Slaton’s parents, her mother is Sarah Gorman who is a single mother of three.

Growing up with three children in college, her mom did full-time jobs and further effort to raise the kids in a proper environment.

Seeing her battle up close, Haley takes her mom as an inspiration and wants to make an example for her own kid as well as her family.
