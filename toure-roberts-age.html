Biography

Toure Roberts was born September 8, 1972, in Oakland, California.  He was raised Christian and disciplined by his father. This strained his father-son bond. His father is famed Bishop Roberts. He guards his mother and siblings’ information.

He admitted in an interview that his father’s strictness affected him.

“My Dad was super hard on me, and I didn’t know how to interpret his strict posture with his passionate desire for me to do well,” Roberts says. It “was confusing, and I got broken by it.”

Touré is most recognised for founding ONE, a global religious and empowerment congregation. (Formerly One Church LA) Founder of the non-profit Artist Resources Centre and philanthropist. He also wrote ‘Wholeness’ and ‘Purpose Awakening.’
