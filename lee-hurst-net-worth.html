His career trajectory is anything but linear. Starting from humble beginnings in London, his venture into comedy was less a calculated leap and more a fortuitous stumble.

This moment marked the inception of a journey that would see him become one of Britain’s cherished comedians.

Hurst’s breakthrough came as a regular panelist on the BBC Two comedy sports quiz They Think It’s All Over, laying the foundation for his television career.

FAQs about Lee Hurst

Who is Lee Hurst?

He is an English comedian, best known for being a regular panelist on the comedy sports quiz They Think It’s All Over from 1995 to 1997. He has also made notable appearances on other television shows and is known for his stand-up comedy.

What are some of hí notable TV appearances?

Besides They Think It’s All Over, he has appeared on shows like Shark Tank, Salvage Squad, The Warehouse, That’s Showbusiness, The Stand Up Show, Have I Got News for You, and hosted Saturday Live.

Does he run a comedy club?

Yes, he runs his own comedy club, Lee Hurst’s Backyard Comedy Club, and focuses much of his time on this venture.

What controversies has he been involved in?

He has faced criticism and Twitter suspensions for making controversial statements and jokes about figures such as Chris Whitty and Greta Thunberg.

What are his views on social and political issues?

He has expressed critical views of the Blair government and Rupert Murdoch’s media, and has controversial opinions on COVID vaccinations and lockdowns.

He once considered running in the 2004 London mayoral election to oppose redevelopment plans affecting his comedy club.

Has he been suspended from Twitter?

Yes, Hurst has been suspended from Twitter multiple times, including for comments about Chris Whitty and Greta Thunberg.

Did he apologize for his controversial comments?

He has shown no regret for his controversial joke about Greta Thunberg, indicating that he aimed for humor rather than offense.

What personal challenges has he faced?

He lives with ankylosing spondylitis, a severe form of arthritis that causes acute back and joint pain.
