Sam Claflin Ex-Wife: Who is Laura Haddock?

Laura Jane Haddock is a British actress and former wife of actor Sam Claflin.

Born on August 21, 1985, Laura is known for appearing as Zoë Walker in White Lines.

She also appeared in the films Honest, Da Vinci’s Demons, Guardians of the Galaxy, The Inbetweeners Movie and  Transformers: The Last Knight.

Laura and Claflin started dating in 2011, after they met while auditioning for My Week with Marilyn.

The duo tied the knots in 2013 and divorced in 2019.

They have a son, and a daughter together.

Who Is Sam Claflin?

Samuel Claflin is an English actor known for appearing as Philip Swift in the 2011 film Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides.

Born on 27 June 1986, in England, Sam graduated from the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art.

In 2013, he played Finnick Odair in The Hunger Games film series and also went on to starr in the romantic film Me Before You.

So far, Sam has starred in several TV series and films such as Adrift, The Nightingale, Peaky Blinders,  Enola Holmes (2020), The Pillars of the Earth, Seventh Son, The Corrupted, the BBC TV drama United, and many others.

Claflin additionally appeared in the fifth season of the BBC television drama Peaky Blinders.

In 2023, he starred as Billy Dunne in Daisy Jones & The Six, which premiered on Amazon Prime Video.
