Kidi Discloses Why He Tattoos His Album Names On His Body

Afrobeat singer Kidi, has been observed to have all his album names on tattooed on his skin.

The reason for this was not widely known till now, that the singer revealed it an interview with Eli Air on a Koforidua based radio station, MY Fm.

It has been a pattern of the ‘Say Cheese’ hit maker to always have the name of his album tattooed on him.

Disclosing the reason for this, the singer said he loves tattoos and in order for him not to regret tattoos he has done, he will prefer to tattoo names of his albums on his skin.
