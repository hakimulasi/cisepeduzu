Who is Greta Lees Husband Russ Armstrong?

Russ Armstrong is the husband of Greta Lee an American actress who has gained recognition for her work in film and television. The couple has been married since 7 September 2014. They have two children.

Greta Lee’s husband Armstrong has been a writer, producer, and actor for many years. He played Jed in “30 Rock” in 2012 before landing a role in the series “Master of None” in 2015.

From 2016 to 2017, Armstrong was the executive producer of 13 episodes of “The Daily Show,” which was eventually taken over by Trevor Noah. He just produced three episodes of “Patriot Act with Hasan Minhaj” in 2020.

During an interview a few years into marriage, Lee revealed that while she and her husband Armstrong are happily married, they attend couple’s therapy for “maintenance.” After becoming parents, Armstrong and his wife relocated 30 minutes away from where she grew up, and vowed never to return, with their kids for a quieter life after over ten years of living in New York.
