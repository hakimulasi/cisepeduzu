How do you breed a Dortal dragon?

To breed a Dortal dragon, you can use a Kwall Dragon and an Iden Dragon, in either order, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed an Eww dragon?

To breed an Eww Dragon, once the event ends, you can use a Dortal Dragon and an Ooze Dragon, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a Loot Dragon?

The Loot Dragon can be bred by using a Gold Dragon and any dragon containing the Plant element, in either order, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed an apocalypse dragon?

The Apocalypse Dragon can be bred using any two dragons, in either order, containing the Plant, Cold, Lightning, and Metal elements, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a rare dragon?

To breed a rare dragon, you can breed 2 hybrids, each with different elements, such as a swamp/storm combination. For better chances at epic and rare dragons, try using 2 hybrid dragons of level 11 and up.

How do you breed a diamond dragon?

The Diamond Dragon can be bred using an Ice Dragon and a Mine Dragon, in either order, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a ghostly earth dragon?

The Ghostly Earth Dragon can only be bred by using two Ghostly Earth Dragons at any Breeding Cave outside the Rift.

How do you breed a nebula dragon?

The Nebula Dragon can only be bred at the Cooperative Breeding Cave. The breeding pair of dragons must contain the Nebula Dragon and any other dragon.

What can I breed a misfortune dragon with?

The Misfortune Dragon can be bred by using any two dragons, in either order, containing the Treasure and Apocalypse elements at any Breeding Cave. Breeding must start during the night.

How do you breed a regift dragon?

The Regift Dragon can be bred using any two dragons, in either order, containing the Plant, Fire, and Cold elements, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a Stygian dragon?

The Stygian Dragon can be bred using any two dragons, in either order, containing the Fire, Metal, and Dark elements, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a Glimmerwing dragon?

The Glimmerwing Dragon can only be bred at the Cooperative Breeding Cave. The breeding pair of dragons must contain the Glimmerwing Dragon and any other dragon.

How do you breed a Kage dragon?

The Kage Dragon can be bred by using an Eldritch Dragon and a Wrath Dragon, in either order, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a Chimneypuff dragon?

The Chimneypuff Dragon can be bred by using any two dragons, in either order, containing the Air and Dark elements at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a dapple thorn dragon?

The Dapplethorn Dragon can be bred with any two dragons containing the Dark and Water elements at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a moonstruck dragon?

The Moonstruck Dragon can be bred using a Love Dragon and a Rose Dragon, in either order, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a trilobite dragon?

The Trilobite Dragon can be bred by using a Rhinotorch Dragon and a Faux Dragon, in either order, at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a plasma dragon?

The Plasma Dragon can be bred by using any two dragons, in either order, containing the Lightning and Water elements at any Breeding Cave.

How do you breed a ghostly Kringle dragon?

The Ghostly Kringle Dragon can be bred using a Ghostly Trinket Dragon and a Ghostly Earth Dragon, in either order, at any Breeding Cave outside the Rift.

How do you breed a permafrost dragon?

The Permafrost Dragon can be bred by using any two dragons, in either order, containing the Earth and Cold elements at any Breeding Cave.

Can you breed a galaxy dragon?

Galaxy dragons can only be bred in the Cooperative Breeding Cave. On July 1, 2015, the Antarian Dragon, Arcturian Dragon, Bizurian Dragon, Polarian Dragon, and the Sorarian Dragon were added to the market.

Can you breed a triple rainbow dragon?

The Triple Rainbow Dragon can be bred using any two dragons, containing at least four different elements in total, at any Breeding Cave.

Can you breed chrysalis dragons?

Chrysalis dragons can be bred by following a specific pattern. Acquire 3 of the 5 base Chrysalis dragons, making sure they are not next to each other in the breeding process.
