Fai Khadra Is Not Dating Kylie Jenner

It made a story when Kylie Jenner posted a maskless picture with Fai Khadra in Paris amid the Coronavirus pandemic. The duo posed on a roof as if no disease is currently ravaging the globe. Jenner shared the picture on her Instagram on 28 August 2020 with her friend Khadra that also brought their dating speculations.

In a couple of pictures, Khadra and Jenner were looking cozy together.

But several sources have come with the statement that they are actually good friends rather than the lovebirds. As per TMZ, sources close to Kylie Jenner mentioned that the 23-year-old billionaire is not dating Fai Khadra. On the contrary, they enclose a close friendship and have been part of each other's small social circle during the pandemic.

Fai Khadra and Kylie Jenner were together during their Utah trip in the 2020 summer.

Fai Khadra and Kylie Jenner in Utah (©: TMZ)

Besides that, Khadra was Kendall Jenner's non-date date in Santa Monica in October 2019, and he was also rumored to be dating Kourtney Kardashian in 2018 after her split from Younes Bendjima.

As of now, Kylie is co-parenting her child with rapper Travis Scott.

Who Is Fai Khadra?

Fai Khadra is an Instagram star with more than 1.4 million followers on his Instagram account. He gained his prominence with his friendship with Kendall Jenner. Besides, he is a singer who released his single called 'Love It.'

Fai Khadra was born in 1991 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, and he celebrates his birthday on 9 September. He grew up with his four siblings, namely, Sama, Haya Abu Khadra, Simi, and Haze Khadra.

Both of his brothers are fashion icons, while his sisters are DJs and fashionistas. His mother, Rula, is a businesswoman who owns the upscale lifestyle boutique The Art of Living in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

Fai Khadra's Relationship

Fai Khadra has been several relationship rumors throughout the years. Back in a few years, he got linked with Victoria's Secret model Devon Windsor after they were spotted together. As per Harpersbazaar, Khadra and Windsor were in a serious relationship. They started dating in 2014 and called off their bond the following year in 2015.

Fai Khadra and his ex-girlfriend, Devon Windsor (©: Elle)

Apart from that, Khadra has been rumored of dating Bella Hadid, Kourtney Kardashian, Rita Ora, and Kendall Jenner.

Fai Khadra and Kendall Jenner were a date during Justin Bieber and Hailey Baldwin's wedding. However, the duo did not officially initiate their relationship.

Concerning the current scenario of Fai Khadra's romantic venture, he appears to be single with no traces of his love interest. He is linked with Kylie Jenner recently, though.
