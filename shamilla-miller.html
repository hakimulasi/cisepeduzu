Shamilla Miller Age, Bio, Net Worth, Boyfriend

Shamilla Miller is a South African actress famous for playing Riley Morgan in Netflix's 'Blood & Water,' Hilda in ETV's 'Z'bondwe,' Chanel in Penguin Films' 'Forced Love 2.' Besides, she has also appeared in movies like The Verdict, Double Echo, Suzie Girl, Zulu, Back to School Mom, etc.

Shamilla was born on September 14, 1988, in Cape Town, South Africa. She is in her mid thirties. From a tender age, she wanted to be an actress and took part in school dramas.
