What is Lauren Daigle's Net Worth?

Lauren Daigle is an American contemporary Christian singer and songwriter who has a net worth of $6 million. Lauren Daigle was born in Lafayette, Louisiana in September 1991. She released the EP How Can It Be in 2014 and it reached #3 on the US Heatseekers chart and #18 on the US Christian chart. Daigle's debut studio album was also titled How Can It Be and was released in 2015.

The album was certified Gold and reached #1 on the US Christian chart and #4 on the Billboard 200 chart. Her second album Behold: A Christmas Collection was released in 2016 and reached #1 on the US Christian chart, #4 on the US Holiday chart, and #29 on the Billboard 200 chart. Daigle's single "Trust in You" Reached #1 on the US Christian chart and her singles "How Can It Be", "First", and "Come Alive (Dry Bones)" all reached the Top 10. Daigle was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Christian Music Album in 2016.
