Biography

Cassidy Gifford was born in New York City, NY on August 2, 1993. Notable for her role as Kara in the 2014 film God’s Not Dead and for her portrayal of Hannah in the 2011 feature Adventures of Serial Buddies, she is also recognized for her appearances in episodes of Blue Bloods and The Suite Life on Deck. In 2015, she began production on Caged No More, co-starring in the film with actors Kevin Sorbo and Cynthia Gibb. She made her screen acting debut in a 2005 episode of That’s So Raven.
