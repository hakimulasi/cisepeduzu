Was Joy Sunday in Black Panther?

Black Panther (Wakanda Forever), a 2022 American superhero film, is based on the Marvel Comics character. The movie was released by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures and was produced by Marvel Studios. It is the 2018 follow-up to Black Panther.

Black Panther’s world debut was on October 26, 2022, at the El Capitan Theatre and the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood (Wakanda Forever). It was subsequently made accessible in the US on November 11, 2022.

The movie is currently the seventh-highest-grossing film of 2022 with around $680 million in worldwide box office receipts. T’Challa, the king of Wakanda, has a disease that his sister Shuri believes the heart-shaped herb will cure.

Was Joy Sunday in Black Panther?

Joy Sunday is an actor, albeit she wasn’t a part of the Black Panther cast. Joy Sunday may be seen in the motion pictures and television programs Good Trouble, Macy, Yas Kween, Alive, and Her Mind in Pieces, among others. As Shuri, the Black Panther, Letitia Wright portrayed her.
