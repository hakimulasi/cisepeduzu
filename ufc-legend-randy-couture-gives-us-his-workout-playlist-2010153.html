UFC Legend Randy Couture Gives Us His Workout Playlist

At 46, Randy Couture (gotta love that name) is the world's oldest UFC fighting champion. But who knew the small-town boy from Oregon who trained to bring martial arts into the mainsteream was also an audiophile?

Well, a guy's gotta build those muscles to something — I recently read that you can increase your workout's effectiveness by 34% by adding the right soundtrack — and he personally selected the tunes found on Don't Quit Music Presents: Randy Couture Mix, Volume 1, that just went on sale on iTunes.

But don't let me do all the talking: Randy himself sent in this exclusive clip to explain it all to you. Check it out!
