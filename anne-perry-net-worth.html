What is Anne Perry's Net Worth?

Anne Perry is an English author who has a net worth of $10 million. Anne Perry was born in Blackheath, London, England in October 1938. She is an author of historical detective fiction and is best known for her Thomas Pitt and William Monk series. Anne Perry was convicted of participating in the murder of her friend's mother at 15 years old in 1954 and served five years and then changed her name. Her Thomas Pitt series began with The Cater Street Hangman in 1979 and has more than 30 books.

Perry's William Monk series began in 1990 and has more than 20 books. She is also known for her The World War I series, The Christmas stories series, The Christmas Collections series, and Timepiece series. Anne Perry won the 2001 Edgar Award for Best Short Story for "Heroes". Her father was physicist Dr. Henry Rainsford Hulme.
