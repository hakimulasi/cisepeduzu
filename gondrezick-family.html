Kysre Gondrezick Family: Who Are The Parents Of A Champion?

Who are in Kysre Gondrezick family? Free agent basketball player Kysre Gondrezick hails from the United States and is now seeking a roster spot. She entered the world on July 27, 1997, and her talents have been on display in the WNBA with the Indiana Fever and the Chicago Sky.

It was at Benton Harbor High School in Michigan that Gondrezick got her start as a basketball player. The 2016 Michigan Miss Basketball and the Michigan Gatorade Player of the Year are just two of the many honors she has received for her extraordinary skill. It’s worth noting that her mother was an integral part of her high school experience as an assistant coach at Benton Harbor.

Kysre Gondrezick continued her basketball career in college, spending time at both the University of Michigan and West Virginia University. Her career advanced dramatically when the Indiana Fever picked her with the fourth overall choice in the 2021 WNBA draft. On January 18th, 2022, however, the Fever declared via their Twitter account that she was no longer a member of the squad.

Despite the setback, Gondrezick was still determined to try out for the 2022 Chicago Sky. She didn’t make the opening night roster since she was waived on May 4.

Gondrezick signed a multiyear endorsement deal with Adidas in April 2021, marking a major milestone in her career and a testament to her marketability and rising prominence both on and off the basketball court.

Who Are Kysre Gondrezick Parents?

As the daughter of Grant Gondrezick, a former NBA player, and Lisa Harvey, a former college player, Kysre Gondrezick is part of a basketball legacy. Her mother, Lisa, won a National Championship at Louisiana Tech, while her father, Grant, played college basketball at Pepperdine.

Kalabrya, Kysre’s sister, is also a notable basketball player; she starred for the women’s basketball team at Michigan State University. The Gondrezicks have an incredible history with the game.
