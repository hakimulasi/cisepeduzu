Alison Sweeney Will Return To NBC's 'Days Of Our Lives'

Alison Sweeney will make her return to NBC's Days Of Our Lives. She will be on the show from this month in Burbank after the show resumes. The story of her Sweeney's return flaunted in August 2020, where she was said to reprise her role as Sami Brady. She joined the remaining cast when they returned to DOOL back on 31 August 2020.

Speaking of Sweeney's return to the show, the executive producer Ken Corday mentioned:

"It is always a beautiful day in Salem when we get to welcome Alison Sweeney back to the canvas. Sami Brady is an integral part of our show’s history, and we are elated that she’s coming home again."

As of September 2020, Sweeney has already finished shooting the episodes of DOOL, which will last from late September. As per the show, her storyline 'will center around family and her recently SORASed daughter Allie Horton (Lindsay Arnold).' And when she does not show up in Salem, she will star and executive produce for Hallmark movies and Mysteries based on the romantic-comedies genre.
