What is Tyson Beckford’s Ethnicity?

Tyson Beckford a popular American model and actor is of mixed ethnicity. He was born to a Jamaican mother, Hillary Dixon Hall and a Panamanian father of Jamaican and Chinese-Jamaican descent, Lloyd Beckford.

Soon after his birth, his mother returned the family to Jamaica, where they resided for seven years until relocating to Rochester, New York, where he went to Pittsford Mendon High School and graduated from there.

What is Tyson Beckford’s Nationality?

Tyson Beckford’s nationality is American because he was born in The Bronx, New York City, USA. In How Many Shows Did Tyson Beckford Appear?

Beckford was approached in 1992 by Erik Lauren Counsel of the hip-hop publication “The Source” in Washington Square Park about appearing in a style piece in the publication New York City.

Ralph Lauren hired Beckford in 1993 to serve as the face of the company’s Polo range of men’s clothing. In 1995, the cable music channel VH1 named Beckford “Man of the Year” and People magazine listed him among the “50 Most Beautiful People in the World.”
