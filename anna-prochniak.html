Heart Parade Actress Anna Próchniak Bio | Wiki

Anna Próchniak is 33 years old Polish Actress who is less known in the Hollywood industry, but in the polish acting industry, she is better known for her acting as Malgosia in Oleg and Sergeant Weronika Dzwonek in “Behawiorysta,” and nowadays, this stunning star has been thrust into the spotlight for playing the lead role of “Magda” in “Heart Parade” in which director Filip Zylber gave her the chance to make a romantic presence alongside “Michal Czernecki.”

Prior to becoming the cast of “Heart Parade” in 2022, Anna made her fabulous landing in Behawiorysta as “Sergeant Weronika Dzwonek” and as Karol’s Mother in Negatyw, but in the same year, her prominence will enhance when her fans watch her in the character of “Magdalena” in Director Caroline Ingvarsson forthcoming film “Unmoored.”

Anna Próchniak Birthday & Early Life

The 5 feet 4 inches tall actress Anna Próchniak was born on December 22, 1988, to her polish parents in Lublin City in Poland, where she began her journey as a contemporary and ballet dancer at a very early age and even provided dance training, but at the age of 22, she decided to switch her career in the acting industry that’s why in 2010 she enrolled in “National Film School in Lódz,” from where her theater journey began.

Career

As a theater artist in 2011, Anna first performed in “Three Sisters,” after that’s she steadily featured in “Natasha’s Dream” &  “King Oedipus,” but while she began her theater journey at the same time, she got the chance to face the camera when director Filip Marczewski gave her the chance to perform the role of “Irmina,” in his film “Shameless,” after that until 2016 she steadily landed in “Lasting,” “True Law,” “Warsaw ’44,” “Obywatel,” “Na krawedzi,” “Teatroteka: Miss HIV,” “Krew z krwi,” “The Innocents” and “Bodo.”

On the one hand, she continued to get work in the industry, but with this, she also won many awards, including Zbyszek Cybulski Award, Piotr Lazarkiewicz Award, and Elle’s Rising Star Award, but the real jump in Próchniak’s acting journey came when she continuously appeared in “Bad Day for the Cut,” “Bodo,” “Belle Epoque,” “The Fastest,” in 2017.
