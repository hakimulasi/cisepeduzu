    Nick Mayorga Facts

  • His self-titled channel Nick Mayorga created on September 5, 2013, has already accumulated more than 200k subscribers with more than 5 million combined views. On the other hand, Nick and Jojo which was created on December 28, 2016, has garnered more than 400k subscribers with more than 10 million combined views.
  • His collaborative channel Free Time which was launched by Tal Fishman and Adi Fishman on April 26, 2014, has already amassed more than 3 million subscribers with more than 150 million combined views. Two of the most viewed videos on this channel are the “Lie Detector Shock Test” and “Prank Calling Without Hearing Them”.
  • He features a wide variety of niches on his channels and keeps a consistent flow of entertaining yet top-quality content on his uploads. Topics featured on all of his platforms include comedy shorts, reaction videos, random vlogs, challenges, Q&A, and many others.
  • Besides being a known collaborator with many popular YouTube artists like Adi and Tal Fishman, which was definitely one of the contributing factors that fueled his popularity on the internet, he was also quick to gain a solid following on Instagram where he tends to often post attractive photos of himself.
  • Before renaming Whatever Time to Nick and Jojo, it was formerly co-hosted by his friend and fellow YouTuber Adi Fishman who made his final appearance in one video called “We Broke Up?!? where he was seen bidding his farewell while Mayorga introduced his new co-host, Joseph Jim. Amidst the changes, Mayorga still features the same type of video content.
