Lauren Conrad's &quot;Party Animal&quot; Halloween Costume Is a Glam Deer

Who let these animals in the house?!

Every year, Lauren Conrad has the best handmade Halloween costume, and 2015 is no different. The Hills alum, 29, took to Instagram on Thursday, Oct. 22, to reveal what she and her friends are dressing up as, and this year, it’s all about that forest life.

“So excited to be a bunch of ‘Party Animals’ this Halloween!!” she wrote, captioning a photo of herself and three of her glammed-up friends (taken by Stephanie Todaro). Front and center, Conrad, who just celebrated her first wedding anniversary with husband William Tell, channels her inner Bambi, rocking handmade antlers (of course!) with fur-topped sandals and an ivory maxi dress.

Equally adorable and fuzzy, her three friends are critters, too, dressing up as a mouse, a cheetah, and a bunny, respectively. (You can get their costume tutorials here.)

Adding a bit more, Conrad wrote via LC.com, “Well ladies, it’s that time of year again: My Halloween costume reveal! Every October I get so excited to get crafty and make a new Halloween costume (homemade ones are always my favorite)… This year, instead of putting together one Halloween costume, I decided to create a group costume for me and my group of girlfriends. Our vision? Equal parts chic and wild.”

That said, the ladies weren’t the only ones who dressed up at Chez Conrad! The designer’s dogs, Chloe and Fitz, got their Halloween on, too, modeling teeny hats as a cop and a robber. Conrad shared a pic of the pups all gussied up, and captioned the shot, “Here comes trouble!”
