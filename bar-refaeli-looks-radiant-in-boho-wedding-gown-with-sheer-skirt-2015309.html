Bar Refaeli Looks Radiant in Boho Wedding Gown With Sheer Skirt

Model behavior! It doesn't take much for Victoria's Secret Angel Bar Refaeli to look stunning in a photo, but her most recent Instagram post takes the (wedding) cake. 

Sharing a snap of herself and her new husband, Israeli businessman (and heir to Israeli food importing company Neto ME Holdings, Ltd.) Adi Ezra, on Wednesday, Sept. 30, Refaeli, 30, showcased her fit figure in a jaw-dropping bohemian, see-through wedding dress by Chloe. Her hair was half up in beachy waves, and her natural makeup highlighted her radiant features. Click here to see more pics of her wedding dress.

In the image, Ezra, 40, stands beside her in a classic white Oxford shirt and navy blue slacks.

The beaming shot was accompanied by an even more joyous caption. "The happiest day of my life!" Leonardo DiCaprio's former love interest wrote. "I married my best friend. Live, Laugh, Love together forever."
