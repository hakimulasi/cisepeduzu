52 Weird Questions to Ask a Girl

Weird questions are questions that enable you to find out just how weird a person is. Almost everyone has a weird side of them that can usually only be known when they are asked weird questions. Weird questions to ask a girl are fun ways to get into the mind of a lady especially if she seems to be a very interesting person. Also, if you want to help your girlfriend or your friend that is a girl crawl out of her shell, weird questions will help you achieve that.

Whether for a date night, a casual text conversation, a truth or dare game session, or a random tête-à-tête, the following 52 weird questions to ask a girl serve the ultimate purpose of creating a funny and engaging conversation. However, it is important to note that you can only ask weird questions to a girl that will be comfortable responding to them. Ensure you let her know that you are about to ask her a weird/crazy/odd or ridiculous question before you go ahead and ask.

Weird Questions to Ask Your Girlfriend

It can be fun and interesting to be able to throw several weird questions at your girlfriend to ascertain her level of weirdness. Asking your girlfriend weird questions can also be another way for the both of you to strengthen your bonds in your relationship as you both can find some weird things in common.

There are several weird questions to ask your girlfriend, here are some worthy examples. Take note, however, that it is only your girlfriend that you can ask the following weird questions.

1. Do you sniff your underwear to find out if they are clean?

This is a weird question that you can only ask your girlfriend if you are sure she will take no offense to it.

2. When was the last time you washed your black bra?

This is a funny weird question and your girlfriend may just laugh it off.

3. Have you ever been caught touching yourself?

This question is weird because if the answer is yes, it makes the whole situation even weirder.

4. What is your weirdest sexual fantasy?

If you want to find out the weird things that go on in the mind of your girlfriend, this is one of the questions to ask.

5. Have you ever suffered an itchy rash on your genitals?

This is top of the list of the very weird questions to ask a girl even if she is your girlfriend.

6. Do you ever cry to get my attention?

Some ladies cry to get their boyfriend’s attention but you don’t expect your girlfriend to admit that she does that too.

7. When was the last time you held a fart around me?

A lot of girls hold in their farts around their boyfriends. If she has never farted around you, we recommend this weird question.

8. What is your most embarrassing first date experience?

You don’t expect your girlfriend to freely talk about her most embarrassing first date experience and that is why this is a weird question.

9. Between a cockroach and a caterpillar, which would you eat if you were forced to?

If you intend to make her feel like puking, this weird question is a good idea.

10. What part of your body do you want to fix the most?

Asking your girlfriend the part of her body she wants to fix the most is weird because it may cause her to feel awkward telling you about it.

18. In a fight, would you rather exchange blows or exchange words?

This is one of the weird questions that will help you know the kind of lady you are texting and her conflict style.

Crazy Questions to Ask a Girl

A crazy question is a funny question that can be described as “out of the blues”. When you ask an unexpected question or a controversial question, it can be said to be a crazy question—especially when the answer to the question may not be so straightforward.

Here are some good examples of crazy questions to ask a girl, that would have her wondering what side of the bed you woke up on.

32. What will happen if the kangaroos in Australia invade Uruguay?

It is said that there are over 40 million kangaroos in Australia while there are slightly over 3 million people in Uruguay. This is a kangaroo invasion.

33. What dinosaur would you love to have as a pet?

Can a dinosaur even be a pet? This magnificent now extinct animal specie do not look like they can ever be pets if they still existed.

34. Why is Greenland iced and Iceland green?

A vast landmass of Greenland is covered in ice while a vast landmass of Iceland is covered in green grass, despite their ironic names. This is a good example of a geographical crazy question to ask a girl.

35. Why are they called hot dogs?

Most people must have wondered why sausages are called hot dogs especially when they aren’t made out of dogs.

36. Why are cars driven in parkways and parked in driveways?

There are so many contradictory words and situations, and this is one of such.

37. Tell me the secret you would never tell anyone

Asking to be told a secret that would never be told to anyone is a crazy thing to do because it will no longer be a secret if she tells you.

38. What is the meaning of hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia?

If you can correctly pronounce this word, then you can ask this question in person, if not, it’s best to ask it over text. The answer to this question is one of the reasons why it is a crazy question because it is somewhat ironic.

39. Why are they called buildings if they are already built?

Another crazy question. If they are already built, why are they still called buildings? Maybe if she has a degree in architecture, she may have an appropriate answer.

40. Would you rather grow a tail on your forehead or have hooves as hands?

This is one of the most hilarious would you rather crazy and weird questions to ask a girl. Hooves for hands or a tail on the forehead?

41. How did the people who invented time know what time it was?

Crazy questions are meant to cause the respondent a little bit of confusion when they try to figure out an answer. This is one of those crazy questions.

Odd Questions to Ask a Girl

When you ask a girl an unusual question or a question that she will regard as strange, then you are asking an odd question. Here are some examples of odd questions to ask a girl.

42. When was the last time you saw your period?

Before you ask a girl this odd question, be sure she is quite comfortable around you and may not feel offended by this question. This is one of the oddest questions to ask any girl.

43. Who lives in a pineapple under the tree?

If she is not a fan of the SpongeBob Squarepants show, this is a very odd question to ask her.

44. Why are you still single?

Top on the list of odd questions to ask a girl. We recommend that you only ask this question to a girl that is your close friend and understands your sense of humor. Otherwise, you may be in for tongue lashing.

45. Do you snore?

This is a very odd question to ask a girl. Do you expect a lady to admit that she snores?

46. What would you do if you found a dead body on your doorstep?

Nobody excepts to see a dead body on their front porch and that is why this is an odd question.

47. Why aren’t cookies cooked and bacon baked?

Why are they called cookies if they are baked and not cooked? And why is bacon cooked instead of baked? Very odd indeed.

48. How often do you lie in a day?

This is a putting her on the spot type of odd question because it is either she admits to lying or lies with her answer.

49. How many admirers do you have?

Asking a girl how many admirers she has is unusual and very odd. She may not be able to give you a reply and may just scoff at you.

50. If you tried to fail and you succeed, did you win or lose?

Funny and witty, this odd question is one of the weird questions to ask a girl, that will have her re-evaluating the meaning of success.

51. Will you go back in time and kill baby Hitler?

This question has been around for a couple of years and has been used as a great conversation starter. Adolf Hitler is famous for his cruelty and crimes. And as such, will you kill a baby because it’s Hitler? Or would you spare Hitler because he is still a baby? Save the world from future doom or kill an innocent baby?

52. What will happen if the only judge in a city committed a crime?

This is top on the list of odd questions you can ask a girl. A judge is meant to determine if an accused person is innocent or guilty. What will happen if that judge himself commits a crime? Will he have to judge himself if he happens to be the only judge?

Weird questions can be a lot of fun as well as helping you get into the mind of your girlfriend or a girl that is your friend. The aforementioned weird questions to ask a girl as well as the list of crazy, odd, and ridiculous questions are great icebreakers and conversation starters that can help you unwind with your partner or friend.
