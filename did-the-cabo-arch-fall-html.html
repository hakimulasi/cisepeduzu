Did the Cabo Arch fall?

The iconic Cabo San Lucas Arch was the first landfall site of hurricane ‘Odile’ in 2014, with rough waters and winds exceeding 140 kilometres per hour causing a drastic change in the local ecosystem, with tonnes of sand being removed, resulting in structural collapses and the eradication of flora and fauna.

What is the name of the arch in Cabo?

Cabo San Lucas’ arch is a remarkable rock feature near the southern extremity of Cabo San Lucas, which is located at the very tip of Mexico’s Baja California Peninsula. Locals name the arch “El Arco,” which translates to “the arch” or “Land’s End” in Spanish.
