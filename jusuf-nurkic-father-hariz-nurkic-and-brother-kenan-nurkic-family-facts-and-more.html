Jusuf Nurkic is a Bosnian-American professional basketball player who currently plays as a center for the NBA’s Portland Trail Blazers. He also represents Bosnia and Herzegovina at the international level.

Jusuf Nurkic was a finalist for the FIBA Europe Young Player of the Year award in 2014. His status as a center for the Trail Blazers has earned him the moniker “beast” or “Bosnian greatest.”

The Chicago Bulls selected Bosnian professional basketball player Jusuf Nurkic in the first round of the 2014 NBA draft.

Following his debut, the basketball player was named to the NBA All-Rookie Second Team.
