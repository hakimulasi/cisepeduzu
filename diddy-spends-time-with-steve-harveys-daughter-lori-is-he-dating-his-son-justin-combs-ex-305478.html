Diddy Spends Time With Steve Harveys Daughter Lori Is He Dating His Son Justin Combs Ex?

Sean “Diddy” Combs has been spotted spending time with Steve Harvey’s daughter Lori Harvey, who is 27-years his junior.

The age difference, as well as Lori’s famous father, are not the only reasons people are talking about the rumored new couple. Lori, age 22, was previously linked to Diddy’s son, Justin Combs age 25.

In the last few months, Diddy and Lori have been hanging out a lot together. They sparked romance rumors back in March, but the 49-year-old rapper quickly shot them down.

Diddy claims the two are old family friends. However, his words are doing very little to squash romance rumors, especially when the two keep adding fuel to the fire.

Diddy is allegedly dating Lori Harvey, who is half his age, and the ex-girlfriend of his own son.

Lori is the daughter of Marjorie Harvey, wife of Steve Harvey. Steve was allegedly having an affair with Marjorie while still married to his 2nd wife.

The “family friends” have been spending a significant amount of time together. They were spotted last week having dinner at Nobu in Malibu, as well as at a reggae festival in Miami Beach a few weeks ago.

Last night in New York City, the two were photographed in coordinating outfits. He wore a white button-down shirt with blue and white pinstripes shorts, as well as blue socks and white sneakers. She was decked out in a white off the shoulder top, with flowy high-rise blue and white pinstriped pants.

If their outing was a date, the possible couple was not alone. Lori’s good pal Elisa Johnson also joined the twosome on their night out. Various photos and videos of the fun evening were shared via Elisa’s Instagram stories.

On all of their various adventures, both Diddy and Lori have been all smiles. They are clearly enjoying spending time together amid the romance rumors. She too has also flat out denied they are a couple, as well as squashed speculation they were engaged.

“I’m not engaged. Stop believing everything y’all read on a blog,” Lori wrote in an Instagram Story a few weeks ago.

Steve Harvey has not yet commented on the rumors surrounding his daughter Lori Harvey dating Sean “Diddy” Combs.

Diddy and Lori are both denying they are in a romance, but as the saying goes a picture is worth a thousand words. Photographs of these two together are doing absolutely nothing to prove they are speaking the truth.
