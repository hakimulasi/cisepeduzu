Teyana Taylor Clarifies Her Decision To Leave Music See Her Video

Just the other day, it’s been revealed that Teyana Taylor is hinting at the fact that she could be walking away from music. Check out the post that she wrote after feeling overlooked.

‘Wow, who did that? Y’all did that! Thank you  I ain’t gone front in times of feeling super under-appreciated as an artist, receiving little to no real push from the “machine”, constantly getting the shorter end of the stick, being overlooked, I mean the list on and on lol,’ Teyana began her post.

Read her complete post to learn what more she had to say.

Now, she’s back with a video in which she explains her decision further. She basically said that the lack of support from her label has her feeling unappreciated, and this is not about the Grammys.
