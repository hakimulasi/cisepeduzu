Early Life

Giada Pamela De Laurentiis was born to Alex De Benedetti and Veronica De Laurentiis on August 22, 1970, in Rome, Italy.

She used to spend a lot of time in the restaurant of her grandfather right from her early childhood. She graduated from Marymount High School, and later earned her BA degree in social anthropology from the University of California, Los Angeles in 1996.

De Laurentiis has also studied at Paris’s Le Cordon Bleu to become a pastry chef.

In 2003, Giada De Laurentiis got married to fashion designer Todd Thompson. They have a child named Jade Marie De Laurentiis-Thompson born on March 2008. The couple separated in 2014, and their divorce was finalized in September 2015.

Age, Height, and Weight

Career

Giada De Laurentiis started her career by working as a professional chef in many restaurants in Los Angeles, including ‘Spago.’ She later worked as a food stylist and has styled for the Food & Wine Magazine.

In 2003, she began the cooking show called ‘Everyday Italian’ on the Food Network. In 2006, she became the host of ‘Behind the Bash.’ Soon she became the host of ‘Giada’s Weekend Getaways.’ She has also made several appearances on ‘The Next Food Network Star’ as a guest judge. In 2008, her show ‘Giada at Home’ got premiered.

De Laurentiis has got her restaurant, which is named as “GIADA” in Las Vegas, Nevada. The restaurant offers everything from the outdoor patio to lounge and dining room with Italian cuisine and many other options.

Apart from being a chef and TV personality, Giada De Laurentiis is also a writer and has penned many books including ‘Giada’s Family Dinners,’ ‘Giada at Home,’ ‘Weeknights with Giada’, and ‘Happy Cooking.’

She has also launched many ranges of products for Target. Her products include utensils, pans, pots, cooking tools, and even food items like pasta and sauces.

In 2014, she lent her voice in ‘Pixie Hollow Bake Off’ and had also voiced as “Paulette” in the animated show’ Handy Manny.’

Awards & Achievements

In 2012, Giada De Laurentiis won the Gracie Awards for Outstanding Host for her show Giada at Home. She has won the 2008 Daytime Emmy Awards in the category of Outstanding Lifestyle Host and has received two other Emmy nominations. She has also been inducted into the Culinary Hall of Fame.
