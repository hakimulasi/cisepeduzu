Chloe Yu (born in 1999) is a 23 years old American-Japanese model and actress represented by Women 360 Management. In 2022, this beautiful appeared in her first ever acting project, “Jane.” It is a Sabrina Jaglom-directed thriller film where Madelaine Petsch played the role of a youngster name Olivia, who faces mental challenges after the death of her friend Jane (Chloe). This CreatorPlus production aired on August 26 in Select AMC theatres.

Modeling & Acting

From a trusted source, it came to our knowledge that it has been more than a decade since Chloe has been doing modeling. Yes, you heard right; this stunning Asian star got into modeling when she was in eighth grade. At first, she worked for printed advertisements; later, she widened her horizon and did ramp walks.

Her face quite frequently appears in beauty magazines, including Harper’s Bazaar. Infact she worked for Marvel’s campaign in 2021. Chloe also does TV advertisements, the latest of which is Maybelline’s Fit Me Aerogel campaign.

In 2019, Yu moved to LA and joined a company named Knucklehead as a production assistant. While doing daily errands as a crew member, she developed a desire to try luck in the acting industry. So, she contacted a local talent manager and began auditioning. Luckily in 2021, her name got finalized for a prominent role in a movie named “Jane,” and her career as an actress took off.
