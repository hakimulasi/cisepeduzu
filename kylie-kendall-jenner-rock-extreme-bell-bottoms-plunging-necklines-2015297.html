Kylie and Kendall Jenner Rock Extreme Bell-Bottoms, Plunging Necklines

Groovy, baby! Kylie and Kendall Jenner stepped out in daring, dark ’70s looks on Tuesday, July 28, while grabbing some tasty frozen yogurt at Go Greek Yogurt in Los Angeles.

Kylie, 17, wore a black crop top paired with black bell-bottom pants in a snakeskin pattern that would make Cher proud. Peeking out beneath those dramatic flares were some strappy, black heeled sandals.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star topped off her look with Dior’s “Technologic” sunglasses, Cartier “Love” bracelets, and a furry 3.1 Phillip Lim bag.
