Askren is known for his pinning ability, massive afro, and his unorthodox wrestling style.

Askren defeated national runner-up Tyrone Lewis in the summer of 2008, in two consecutive matches by decision at the US Olympic Trials. It led to him becoming the second student of the University of Missouri to qualify for the Olympics.
